Overview

During my time as an astropyhsics reasearcher at Illinois Tech, I conducted original research into stellar binarity and am the primary author of a forthcoming publication exploring the classification of Sub-Subgiants. I analyzed stellar spectra using Python to derive radial velocity curves for 98 binary systems, extracting orbital and dynamical parameters. To complement observational data, I built Monte Carlo simulations of synthetic stellar populations, directly comparing modeled orbital distributions with those observed. This work strengthened my skills in computational astrophysics, statistical modeling, and scientific writing.

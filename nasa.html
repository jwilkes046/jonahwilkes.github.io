Overview

NASA L'SPACE is a nationally competitive NASA workforce development program focused on mission concept development and technical proposal evaluation. Through team-based projects, participants gain hands-on experience in systems engineering, mission design, and professional technical communication aligned with real NASA review processes.

Mission Concept Academy (MCA)

Participated in the Mission Concept Academy, a semester-long program centered on the formulation of a complete space mission concept in response to a NASA-style solicitation. Worked within a multidisciplinary team to define mission objectives, develop system architectures, perform trade studies, and produce formal design documentation.

  • Developed mission objectives, requirements, and system-level trades
  • Contributed to subsystem definitions and interfaces
  • Collaborated across engineering disciplines in a structured design environment
  • Produced technical documentation aligned with NASA standards

NASA Proposal Writing Evaluation Experience (NPWEE)

Completed the NASA Proposal Writing and Evaluation Experience, focused on understanding how NASA mission proposals are structured, reviewed, and evaluated. Gained insight into evaluation criteria, strengths and weaknesses identification, and the proposal review lifecycle from a reviewer’s perspective.

  • Reviewed and evaluated mock NASA proposals using formal scoring rubrics
  • Identified technical, management, and cost-related proposal risks
  • Practiced professional technical critique and panel-style discussion
  • Strengthened understanding of proposal competitiveness and clarity

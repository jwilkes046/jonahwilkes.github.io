Overview

NASA L'SPACE is a nationally competitive NASA workforce development program focused on mission concept development and technical proposal evaluation. Through team-based projects, participants gain hands-on experience in systems engineering, mission design, and professional technical communication aligned with real NASA review processes.

Mission Concept Academy (MCA)

As the primary Mechanical Engineer on a NASA L’SPACE Mission Concept Academy team, I led the mechanical design of a Mars Rover mission concept developed under NASA systems engineering standards. I produced a detailed rover CAD model in Siemens NX, integrating mechanical structure with thermal, payload, power, and communications subsystems, and worked closely with cross-disciplinary teams to ensure interface compatibility and feasibility.

In addition to design work, I led the development of the mission Concept of Operations (ConOps), defining four distinct operational phases and coordinating subsystem requirements across the mission lifecycle. I supported the project through Preliminary Design Review (PDR) and presented technical results to a NASA Standing Review Board, gaining direct experience with NASA-style design reviews, documentation standards, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

NASA Proposal Writing Evaluation Experience (NPWEE)

As a Mission Designer in the NASA Proposal Writing and Evaluation Experience (NPWEE), I contributed to the development of a full NASA-style mission proposal within a multidisciplinary team. My work included mission formulation support and authorship of technical proposal sections, with an emphasis on clarity, feasibility, and alignment with NASA solicitation requirements.

At the end of this program, I served as a reviewer for peer team proposals, evaluating submissions using formal NASA review rubrics in a Standing Review Board format. This role provided hands-on exposure to NASA’s proposal evaluation process, strengthening my understanding of how technical merit, risk, and communication quality influence proposal competitiveness.
